Supporters: Exhibitors and Sponsors

The support of many excellent sponsors and exhibitors help make SIGCSE the amazing conference that it is.

Exhibitors are an essential part of the SIGCSE community. Whether you are a publisher, vendor, community rep, or evangelist engineer, we want you at SIGSCE, helping us bring the best tools and technologies to the next generation of computing experts.

Sponsors make this conference possible. The best way to transform how we approach the teaching of computing is to bring people together to share best practices, be they design strategies for active learning assessment strategies for education online.
